1. Click the “Settings” cog icon in the upper-right corner of the Superset web page.  
2. Select the menu item labeled “Annotation layers” from the dropdown; the “Annotation Layers” list view opens.  
3. Click the blue “+ ANNOTATION LAYER” button in the top-right corner of the list; a modal titled “Add Annotation Layer” appears.  
4. Type Myannotation into the field labeled “Annotation Layer Name”.  
5. Click the “ADD” button at the bottom of the modal; the dialog closes and the list view reappears.  
6. Verify the creation by locating the new row whose “Layer name” column shows Myannotation; confirm the Status column displays “Available”.
